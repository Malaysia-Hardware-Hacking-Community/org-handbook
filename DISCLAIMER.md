# Legal and Ethical Disclaimer

**Effective Date:** [fill in]
**Applies To:** All M0DUL0 channels, documents, discussions, events, and related activities.

## 1) Educational Scope

M0DUL0 is a hardware security learning community open to anyone. Content is provided for:

- education,
- ethical research,
- lawful skill development,
- hardware modification, and
- CTF participation and preparation.

M0DUL0 is not an operational tasking body and does not direct members to conduct research on specific targets.

## 2) No Authorisation

Nothing in M0DUL0 content, discussion, or participation authorises any person to:

- access, modify, or extract data from devices, firmware, or systems without the owner's permission,
- transmit on RF frequencies without appropriate licences or class assignments,
- bypass security controls on devices or networks they don't own,
- harass, intimidate, or harm any person,
- conduct unlawful surveillance or invasive data collection, or
- weaponise or deploy research against production systems.

References to techniques, tools, and PoCs are educational only and must not be treated as permission to act.

## 3) Legal and Compliance Sensitivity (Malaysia)

Members are responsible for understanding and complying with applicable Malaysian law in their own context, including:

- **Computer Crimes Act 1997 [Act 563]**, covering unauthorised access to and modification of computer material, which extends to firmware and embedded systems.
- **Communications and Multimedia Act 1998 [Act 588]**, governing radio spectrum use relevant to RF and SDR work.
- **Copyright Act 1987 [Act 332]**, relevant to firmware decompilation and reverse engineering.
- **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 [Act 709]** and the **Personal Data Protection (Amendment) Act 2024**, where research surfaces or involves personal data.

This list is illustrative, not exhaustive. Legal obligations such as reporting, notification, and consent handling remain the individual member's responsibility.

## 4) No Legal Advice

M0DUL0 materials do not constitute legal, compliance, or professional advice. If you need legal interpretation, seek a qualified Malaysian advocate and solicitor.

## 5) Individual Responsibility and Liability

Each member is solely responsible for their own research decisions, conduct, and legal compliance. To the fullest extent appropriate, M0DUL0 moderators and contributors do not accept responsibility for member misuse, unlawful conduct, or consequences resulting from independent actions.

## 6) Participation and Enforcement

M0DUL0 may restrict, suspend, or remove participation where behaviour creates legal, ethical, safety, or community risk. Enforcement decisions are made by Volunteers and the Core Founding Team.

Participation in M0DUL0 indicates acknowledgement of this Disclaimer together with [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and related policy documents.
